A short Bio of Rayn Smid


Rayn Smid (born 26 March 1992 in Cape Town), is a South African Rugby Union player, who currently plays for Ealing Trailfinders. His regular position is number eight or flanker.


Rayn went to Rondebosch Boys' High in Cape Town, South Africa, where he started his Rugby Journey. He represented Western Province at age-group level before leavimg school to become a professional Rugby Player.
