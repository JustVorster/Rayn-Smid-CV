Rayn's time at Ealing Trailfinders

In January 2017 Smid joined the RFU Championship side Ealing Trailfinders, where he currently plays. Smid has enjoyed great success with the Trailfinders, making over 100 appearances as well as appearing in almost every match since joining in 2017, amost all of them as captain. He was named to The Rugby Paper's Championship Dream Team for the 2018/19 season after scoring 11 tries.
